Photos Of The RHOA Star PICS Hollywood Life

Phaedra Parks wore a red dress with silver jewels while out and about in New York, NY  on December 14, 2015. 

Phaedra Parks is an entertainment attorney and reality star from Atlanta, Georgia. She is the Managing Partner of The Parks Group, P.C. — a national boutique law firm that represents clients, including entertainers and athletes. Phaedra is also a best-selling author, funeral director and entrepreneur. She rose to fame after joining the cast of ‘The Real Housewives of Atlanta’ for its third season, which premiered on Bravo on October 4, 2010. She was a main cast member from season three until season nine when she was fired after controversial drug allegations. Phaedra admitted during the ‘RHOA’ season 9 reunion that she was the one who told Porsha Williams about Kandi Burruss and husband Todd Tucker’s alleged plan to drug Porsha and have sex with her. Phaedra shares sons Ayden and Dylan with ex-husband Apollo Nida. Phaedra and Apollo, who wed in 2009, finalized their long and messy divorce in November 2016.  Phaedra filed for divorce in 2014, just one month after Apollo began his initial eight-year prison sentence for fraud.
