Milan Lucic is a Canadian professional ice hockey player with an estimated net worth of $22.5 million.

Milan Lucic played minor hockey for Vancouver Minor Hockey Association. Eventually, he joined the Delta Ice Hawks and after five games with the team, he moved to Coquitlam Express.

His junior career continued with the Express and eventually, he played for the WHL's Vancouver Giants. He appeared in 62 games, with 19 points and added 18 points during the playoff games which won them the WHL title. Lucic was selected as the second round, 50th overall pick by the Boston Bruins during the 2006 NHL Entry Draft.

During his stint with the Bruins, he received the Eddie Shore Award in 2009 and 2015, and Seventh Player Award in 2008. On June 26, 2015, he was traded to Los Angeles Kings.

Milan Lucic was born June 7, 1988 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.
