Christian Ponder Bio (Wiki)

Who is Christian Ponder?

Christian Ponder is an American football quarterback. He gained recognition playing for Minnesota Vikings as a 12th overall pick in the 2011 NFL Draft. Currently, he is not under any contract and is a free agent.

Christian Ponder: Bio, Age, Early Life, Childhood, Education

Christian Ponder was born to David Ponder as his father on February 25, 1988. He was born in Dallas, Texas U.S. His father David was also a player in his college days.

In an interview, he revealed that his father influenced him a lot to make a career in Football. As a result, he developed an interest in it and is currently having a very successful career.

Talking about his education, he completed his high school graduation from Colleyville Heritage High School. Afterward, he enrolled in Florida State becoming their most valuable offensive player.

Christian Ponder: Professional Life, Career

As a result of his exceptional display in his college career, Christian Ponder was drafted by NFL in 2011 in the first round by Minnesota Vikings. Eventually, he earned his first victory against the Carolina Panthers in October 2011. In that game, he threw for 236 yards with a pass rating of 102.7.

Afterward, he left Minnesota Vikings to sign with Oakland Raiders in March 2015. In the same year he in November for McGloin. Subsequently, he joined the team such as Philadelphia Eagles, Tennessee Titans. Eventually, he signed a contract with Denver Broncos in November 2015.

Moreover, I signed a one-year contract with San Francisco 49ers in August 2016. Currently, he is a free agent waiting to sign a contract and join a team.
