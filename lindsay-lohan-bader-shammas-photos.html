Photos Of The Married Couple Hollywood Life

Lindsay Lohan finally met her match with Bader Shammas. The 36-year-old has been married to Bader since April 2022, however, she did not announce the marriage until July of that year via Instagram.

“I am the luckiest woman in the world. You found me and knew that I wanted to find happiness and grace, all at the same time,” Lindsay captioned the selfie. “I am stunned that you are my husband. My life and my everything…every woman should feel like this everyday,” she added, with a heart emoji.

Lindsay and Bader were over the moon when they revealed they were expecting a child together on Mar. 14, 2023. Talking to TMZ, they said, “We are very excited for our new family member to arrive and we are looking forward to this next chapter of our lives!”

The pair are clearly too cute. They looked so in love while outside a pub in London on Aug. 29, 2022. The Falling for Christmas star rocked a floral blouse, jeans, and minimal makeup for the outing. Her hubby, for his part, opted for a casual look as well with jeans, a brown t-shirt, and a green bomber jacket.
