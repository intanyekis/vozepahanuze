Welsh Actor Michael Sheen Returns Order Of The British Empire Honor

published on August 08, 2024
Welsh actor Michael Sheen has returned his Order of the British Empire (OBE) honor given to him by Queen Elizabeth due to his opposition to the monarchy. The actor, who received the OBE in 2009 for services to drama, objects to the practice of bestowing the title of Prince of Wales to the heir apparent to the English throne. In an online interview, Sheen said he decided it would be hypocritical to keep the honor after considering the history of Wales and its centuries-old practice of simply handing the title of Prince of Wales to the heir apparent to the English throne.
