Melissa Bagley Obituary: Lynn woman drowns in Franconia Falls in attempt to save 10-year-old son

Melissa Bagley Obituary – Cause of Death: Lynn woman drowns in Franconia Falls in attempt to save 10-year-old son.

In a tragic incident, Melissa Bagley, a 44-year-old mother hailing from Lynn and married to a police officer, lost her life while attempting to rescue her 10-year-old son in New Hampshire.

According to investigators, Bagley’s family and a friend embarked on a walk to Franconia Falls on Tuesday afternoon. Tragically, during the excursion, the young boy slipped and fell into the water. In a daring act of bravery, the mother fearlessly plunged into the treacherous river to rescue her stranded child. The young boy found himself trapped, unable to escape the powerful current that held him captive. With unwavering determination, the mother defied all odds, risking her own safety to save her beloved child. In a display of familial support, two of her other children swiftly intervened as she encountered difficulties.

Everett Police Lt. Sean Bagley discovered his wife on a rock and swiftly initiated CPR in an attempt to save her life. Regrettably, despite his valiant efforts, she succumbed to her injuries.

In a remarkable display of courage and quick thinking, a father successfully rescued his 18-year-old son from a perilous situation. The teenager was subsequently transported to Littleton Hospital for medical treatment.

The Everett Police Department is sad to report the sudden passing of Melissa Bagley 44 yrs of age,wife of Lt Sean Bagley who died during a family outing along the river in the White Mountain area yesterday afternoon. Melissa drown while trying to aid one of her children who slipped into the river.
While we appreciate all those who have reached out we ask that Lt Bagley and his family be given the time and space to mourn the death of Melissa. Thank you, Everett MA Police wrote in a statement shared on Facebook.
