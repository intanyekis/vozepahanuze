CNY Cosmetic &amp; Reconstructive Surgery Introduces BodyTite and FaceTite for Fat Reduction and Skin Ti

SYRACUSE, N.Y., Oct. 29, 2018 /PRNewswire-PRWeb/ -- Drs. Gregory Baum and Anthony Deboni have expanded their leading selection of aesthetic treatments to include BodyTite and FaceTite, an advanced form of radiofrequency-assisted lipolysis (RFAL). RFAL offers patients many advantages, including:

  • precise fat reduction
  • skin tightening
  • immediate improvements
  • minimal scarring

BodyTite and FaceTite represent a significant advancement in liposuction because they enable doctors to remove excess fat and tighten loose skin simultaneously for significant body contouring improvements with minimal scarring. BodyTite and FaceTite are ideal fat reduction treatments for almost any area of the body, including the chin, belly, arms, thighs, and abdomen. About CNY Cosmetic & Reconstructive Surgery
Trust CNY Cosmetic & Reconstructive Surgery in East Syracuse, NY for the highest standard of excellence in cosmetic surgery and one of the most comprehensive selections of procedures and aesthetic treatments in Central New York. Thousands of satisfied clients have received the widely acclaimed and highly personalized care of CNY Cosmetic & Plastic Surgery, led by board-certified cosmetic surgeons Drs. Anthony Deboni and Gregory Baum.

Drs. Baum and Deboni are two of the most highly respected and trusted plastic surgeons in Central New York. Both doctors attended medical school at the SUNY Health Science Center in Syracuse, where they also completed their general surgery training. They traveled to well-respected universities to complete their plastic surgical training and brought that training, knowledge, and experience back to Central New York. With years of surgical experience, they are well-versed in the most effective and leading cosmetic techniques.

About Dr. Anthony Deboni
Dr. Anthony Deboni is a recognized expert in cosmetic and reconstructive surgery, with particular interest and experience in aesthetic surgery of the breast and body contouring. His notable accomplishments include board certification with the American Board of Plastic Surgery, membership with the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, and serving as a clinical instructor of surgery at Upstate University Hospital in Syracuse, NY and clinical instructor of otolaryngology at Upstate University Hospital, Syracuse, NY.

Dr. Deboni is a recognized leader in breast and body aesthetic surgery. In 1997, he was the first in the area to be awarded appointment to the National Advisory Council for Leaders in Breast Aesthetics and was invited by Allergan Medical to advise on advancing the science in breast aesthetics. He came to Syracuse in 1998 and quickly established his expertise in breast surgery, being the first to perform the microsurgical TRAM flap breast reconstruction procedure. Dr. Deboni rapidly became known as a regional expert in breast and cosmetic surgery, and was invited to speak at a several regional and national conferences.

About Dr. Gregory Baum
Dr. Gregory Baum is a recognized local and regional expert in both cosmetic and reconstructive surgery of the face and body. He has extensive experience in cosmetic surgery of the breast and body contouring procedures. Dr. Baum's numerous accomplishments include board certification by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, membership with the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, past appointment as chief of plastic surgery at St. Joseph's Hospital in Syracuse, NY, and serving as a clinical instructor of surgery at SUNY-Upstate Medical University, in Syracuse, NY.

Dr. Baum has been a guest lecturer on many topics related to cosmetic surgery of the breasts, breast reconstruction, skin cancer treatment, and skin care. He was awarded the American Red Cross "Real Heroes" award in 2007. He was appointed to the National Advisory Council for Leaders in Breast Aesthetics in 2012 by Allergan. He was sought after for his advice in advancing this science of breast aesthetics; participation in this national forum was by invitation only and was based on overall excellence in plastic surgery and a commitment to superior outcomes. In 2017, Dr. Baum was invited to sit on an advisory panel of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons regarding cosmetic and reconstructive eyelid procedures.
