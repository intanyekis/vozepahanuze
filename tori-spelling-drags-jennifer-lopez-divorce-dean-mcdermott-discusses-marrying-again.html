Tori Spelling Drags Jennifer Lopez Into Divorce From Dean McDermott As She Discusses Marrying Again

Highlights

  • Tori Spelling is open to love again after her divorce from Dean McDermott, inspired by Jennifer Lopez and Elizabeth Taylor.
  • The actress candidly discussed marrying again on her podcast, even referencing other celebrities who have married multiple times.
  • Tori and Dean do not have a prenup, adding complexity to their divorce settlement as they work through their separation.

Tori Spelling is currently navigating her divorce from Dean McDermott, but she admits she’s still open to finding love again. As Tori says, if Jennifer Lopez can get hitched multiple times, why can’t she?

In the second episode of her "misSPELLING" podcast, the actress was questioned about the possibility of marrying again following her recent divorce filing from McDermott, 57, at the end of last month, after reportedly separating last year.

Why Tori Thinks She’ll Get Married Again Like J.Lo

“I do love planning a wedding. J. Lo did it how many times? Four times? There you go. Sure,” Spelling, 50, candidly said.

Lopez is presently wedded to Ben Affleck. The two married in 2022 after rekindling their relationship nearly two decades after calling off their original engagement. Before him, she was married to Ojani Noa in the late 90s, Cris Judd in the early 2000s, and Marc Anthony, with whom she shares twins, from 2004 to 2011. Their divorce was finalized in 2014.

Selling also referenced another famous celebrity, adding. “Elizabeth Taylor, how many times? Eight. Sure. Why not?”

“You say this all the time. … ‘Be with yourself. Be happy with yourself.’ I don’t want to be with myself,” she continued. “I haven’t been alone, honestly, I still don’t poop alone. Beau [her 7-year-old son] still stands there and stares and talks to me while I’m pooping. It’s like, I don’t know. I haven’t pooped, peed alone in 18 years. First it was Dean, then it was kids. I don’t know. I think I function better with people. Is that codependent? … Cool. Redefine it.”

Tori And Dean Reportedly Don’t Have A Prenup

Dean was the first to announce his separation from Tori in June 2023. “It’s with great sadness and a very very heavy heart that after 18 years together and 5 amazing children, that @torispelling and I have decided to go our separate ways and start a new journey of our own,” he wrote via Instagram.

“We will continue to work together as loving parents and guide and love our children through this difficult time,” he went on. “We ask that you all respect our privacy as we take this time to surround our family with love and work our way through this. Thank you all for your support and kindness.”

After the news broke he moved in with a new girlfriend, Tori filed for divorce from Dean at the end of March. They had previously tied the knot in 2006 following an affair during the production of a Lifetime movie.

Prior to McDermott, Spelling was married to Charlie Shanian in 2004, but their union lasted only 15 months before her relationship with McDermott began; they share an adult son together. At the time, McDermott was married to Mary Jo Eustace for 13 years. Together, Dean and Tori have five children: Liam, 17, Stella, 15, Hattie, 12, Finn, 11, and Beau, 7.

Tori and Dean’s divorce remains ongoing, with various reports indicating they don’t have a prenup, which may complicate their settlement.
