See Photos of the Mob Wives Star Hollywood Life

Renee Graziano has had quite an intensive and interesting life and career trajectory. Her dad Anthony Graziano was a consigliere for the Bonanno crime family. Anthony was arrested on racketeering charges and served time in prison from 2003 to 2011, partially from evidence that Renee’s ex-husband Hector Pagan Jr. provided to police.

Renee was a star of ‘Mob Wives’ from 2011 to 2016, and afterwards she appeared on ‘Celebrity Big Brother,’ and came third for the season she competed in. She was also featured in a 2017 season of ‘Marriage Boot Camp,’ with her partner Joey Gambino. Other than her reality TV appearances, Renee has written two books, which were both released in 2014. One of her books is a cookbook: ‘How To Use A Meat Cleaver: Secrets And Recipes From a Mob Family’s Kitchen.’ The other is a novel, titled ‘Playing With Fire.’
