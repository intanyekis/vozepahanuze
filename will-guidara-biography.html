Who is Will Guidara?

Will Guidara is a restaurateur based in New York City. Will Guidara is the co-owner of the hospitality group Make It Nice, established in 2011, which owns and operates Eleven Madison Park, NoMad New York, NoMad Los Angeles, NoMad Bar, and Made Nice.

Will Guidara: Professional life, Career

Will Guidara initially began his dining room career at Wolfgang Puck’s Spago in Beverly Hills. Additionally, he worked at Tabla, Cafe 2, and the restaurants at the Museum of Modern Art up until 2005.

In 2005, Guidara became the general manager of Eleven Madison Park, where he and Chef Daniel Humm, spearheaded the transformation of the restaurant from a French brasserie to a fine dining destination. Eventually, in 2011, he and Humm purchased the restaurant from Union Square Hospitality Group.

Will and Humm opened the critically acclaimed NoMad New York in 2012 and the separate NoMad Bar in 2014. Furthermore, in April 2017, Guidara and Humm opened Made Nice, a fast-casual restaurant in New York’s Nomad neighborhood. In addition, in 2018, they opened NoMad Los Angeles, their first restaurant outside of New York City.

In addition, Will has also been a guest speaker at the 92nd Street Y, Mistura, and Mesoamerica culinary festivals, Google, Cornell University, and The Culinary Institute of America.

Apart from his career in the restaurant business, he is also an author. He is the co-author of four books with Chef Daniel Humm.

Will’s restaurant has received numerous awards over the years. In 2008, 2014, and 2016, Eleven Madison Park won the James Beard Foundation Award for Outstanding Service.

Similarly, it has also been on the list of The World’s 50 Best Restaurants several times.
