Elon Musk's 'Cybershoes' Are Driving Fans Crazy!

Elon Musk is full of surprises. Only last week, he left the world stumped when he released his new track, “Don’t Doubt Ur Vibe.” Now, he’s stepping out with girlfriend Grimes, in whacky cyber shoes... Musk was actually photoshopped into the footwear.

Truck-spiration

Many have compared the cyber shoes to the recently revealed Tesla Cybertruck, a tank-like electric truck that looks like it belongs on another planet... much like the shoes.

In fact, Musk’s 'cyber shoes' are garnering as much attention as the Cybertruck, both good and bad. You may remember how much mockery revolved around the Cybertruck, owing to its bizarre appearance. And let’s not forget that it also failed its shatterproof glass test live on stage.

Musk wasn’t sure of its success initially, and we’re guessing he’s feeling the same about these whacky shoes too.

“A lot of reasons why people buy pickup trucks in the U.S. is because it’s the most badass truck,” Musk said in a podcast. “Which one’s the toughest truck? What’s tougher than a truck? A tank. A tank from the future.”

You Know What’s Even Tougher?

Tank shoes! His feet look like nothing we’ve ever seen before. And while they may have earned some mockery among Reddit users, there’s still a fair share of people who can’t get enough of these marvelous creations.

In a matter of 2 weeks, Musk has added Musician, songwriter and fashion muse to his resume. Maybe even shoe designer.

You never know, maybe soon you’ll be seeing these cyber shoes in stores.
