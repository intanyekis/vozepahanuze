American singer-songwriter and musician, Adam Noah Levine has an estimated net worth of $50 million.

He is best known as the front man and guitarist for the pop rock band, Maroon 5 and also a coach on the American talent show, The Voice since Season 1. Levine earns $6 million per season as one of the three celebrity hosts of NBC’s The Voice alongside Blake Shelton and Christina Aguilera. Levine has collaborated with several artists such as Ying Yang, Kanye West, Alicia Keys and more.

Levine was born on March 18, 1979, in Los Angeles, California. He married Victoria's Secret model Behati Prinsloo on July 19, 2014.
