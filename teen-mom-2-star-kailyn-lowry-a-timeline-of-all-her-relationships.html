A Timeline Of All Her Relationships

Dedicated fans of the hit MTV reality show, Teen Mom, follow the lives of several young mothers. These famous moms have been navigating the trials and tribulations that come with parenthood...and anything else that life throws at them...for over a decade! The series had a spinoff, titled Teen Mom 2, in late 2010. This spinoff introduced a new group of young mothers, one of whom was Kailyn Lowry.

Since completing nine seasons on Teen Mom 2, Kailyn has given us a personal look into her life. She is a mother of three sons, from three different "baby daddies."

In this article, we will take a closer look at all the relationships and romantic links she has had throughout the history of the show. While some of them lasted longer than others, none were short on drama. Stick around until the end of the list â€” some tea wasn't spilled on the show, so there are surprising facts to discover!

15 It All Begins With '16 And Pregnant'

In 2010, Kailyn was cast in the second season of MTV's 16 and Pregnant. We were introduced to a pregnant, 16-year-old Kailyn. She was expecting a child with her high school sweetheart, Jo Rivera. The new parents welcomed a son to the world, and they named their baby Isaac Rivera. We watched Isaac grow up on Teen Mom 2, right in front of our eyes!

14 Kailyn's Relationship With Jo Rivera Goes Through A Lot Of Ups And Downs

After Isaac's birth, Kailyn and Jo begin experiencing relationship strain, from the stress of adjusting to being new parents. It all proved to be too much because it led the pair to go their separate ways. While they were no longer a couple, they still co-parented for the sake of their son.

13 After Her Relationship With Jo Ends, Kailyn Begins Dating Jordan Wenner

Shortly after her relationship with Jo ended, Kailyn began dating her co-worker at the time, Jordan Wenner. Kailyn dated Jordan for nearly a year before the pair called it quits.

One reason things didn't work out with Jordan was the fact that he became caught up in the attention that the show was getting. He got into fame and lost sight of what was more important - their relationship.

12 Kailyn And Jo Rekindle Their Romance

Years later, Kailyn came clean and confessed to her fans that another reason why her relationship with Jordan abruptly ended was that she cheated on him with her ex, Jo.

Kailyn and Jo's rekindled romance was short-lived and ended with both admitting that it was a bad idea...nonetheless, the pair were on good terms.

11 Kailyn Meets Javi Marroquin And Shortly After Ties The Knot

In 2012, Kailyn confirms her relationship with Javi Marroquin, whom she met while working at a mall. The sparks seemed to fly instantly for these two lovebirds and it wasn't long before they decided to tie the knot in a private ceremony.

Later, they married once again, in front of MTV cameras, This was fun for her fans to see.

10 Kailyn And Javi Welcome A Child Together

There was no hiding this news from her fans - her growing baby bump was hard to miss! In 2013, the family of three became a family of four, with the birth of Kailyn and Javi's son, Lincoln. We saw on the show that Kailyn's first son, Isaac, was very happy about becoming a big brother for the first time.

9 Their Seemingly Perfect Marriage Soon Begins To Fall Apart

A few years into their marriage, Javi was deployed out of the country for military service. We saw on the show that the long-distance thing proved challenging for Kailyn. She felt like a single mother raising two children alone. This led to Kailyn and Javi's divorce after three years of marriage.

8 Rumours Of A Relationship Between Kailyn And Her Friend, Becky Hayter, Emerge During Her Marriage To Javi

These rumors proved to be partially true, as Kailyn admitted to hooking up with Becky during her relationship with Javi, but later claimed that it wasn't serious. She said it was all in good fun.

Kailyn's struggle with determining her sexuality was also said to have caused rifts in her marriage.

7 Kailyn Becomes Romantically Linked To Chris Lopez

Not long after her divorce from Javi, Kailyn began dating a classmate from Delaware State College. Fans were shocked when Kailyn announced her pregnancy with baby number three...and chose to keep the identity of the father private. She later revealed that the father of her son, Lux, was Chris Lopez.

6 During A Break From Her Relationship With Chris, Kailyn Introduces Dominique 'Dom' Potter As Her Girlfriend

If you're having trouble keeping up, get ready - it's about to get a lot messier! In 2018, Kailyn introduced her new girlfriend, Dom Potter. The friends-turned-lovers seemed to be very close during the show. Dom regularly joined family outings and spent quality time with Kailyn's three kids.

5 The Two Seem To Have A Lot Of Fun Together, But Their Relationship Is Short-Lived

Things took a turn for the worse and the pair parted ways. It wasn't clear why at first, but Dom didn't hold back when it came to throwing shade Kailyn's way on Twitter. Fans weren't kept in the dark for too long, though. It was revealed in the Teen Mom 2 aftershow that Kailyn cheated on Dom with Javi.

4 Kailyn Is Seen Once Again With Her On-Again Off-Again Boyfriend Chris

These two sure can't stay away from one another! Here, we see Kailyn and Chris getting cozy for a picture at a baseball game. On the show, we see their co-parenting struggles. There were some concerns about Chris's involvement in his son's life. Fans were worried about what this meant for the family's future.

3 Despite Their Shaky Relationship, They Are Always There For Their Son

Although Kailyn and Chris are still working on their relationship, one thing is for sure - Lux and his happiness always come first! Judging by their smiles in this photo, both parents were certainly joyful while spending time together as a family. This pic was taken on Lux's first birthday.

2 During Their Appearance At The 2019 MTV VMA's, Kailyn and 'Teen Mom 2' Co-Star Leah Messer Spark Relationship Rumours

This is a rumor that drove the fandom into a frenzy! It all started when Kailyn and Leah decided to attend the red carpet together. In one of her Twitter posts, Kailyn describes Leah as her girlfriend. We'd like to see it!

Sadly, Kailyn dispelled the rumors, saying that they are only close friends.

1 Kailyn Announces Her Fourth Pregnancy To The World

It seems as though Kailyn enjoys surprising her fans, as she announced her fourth pregnancy in this cute photoshoot surrounded by her three sons. The father of her fourth child, Chris, hinted to fans on his Instagram that they're expecting a "Young King." Looks like it may be another baby boy for Kailyn!
