Zoey Stark And Gigi Dolin Qualify For NXT Womens Title Ladder Match At NXT Stand And Deliver

Two WWE NXT stars have qualified for NXT Stand and Deliver.

In the wake of reigning champion Roxanne Perez’s uncertain medical status, WWE began holding qualifying matches on March 14 to determine who would compete in a Ladder Match for the NXT Women’s Championship at Stand and Deliver on April 1.

In the first qualifying bout, Zoey Stark emerged victorious over Sol Ruca. Later on, Gigi Dolin pinned Kiana James to earn her spot in the bout. The first two spots are filled, but it remains to be seen how many more competitors will qualify over the next two weeks.

It is currently unclear whether Perez will compete in the Ladder Match.

Below is the updated lineup for NXT Stand and Deliver:

  • : Bron Breakker (c) vs. Carmelo Hayes
  • : Zoey Stark vs. Gigi Dolin vs. TBD
  • : Wes Lee (c) vs. Four opponents chosen by Lee
  • : Kiana James & Fallon Henley (c) vs. Isla Dawn & Alba Fyre
  • Johnny Gargano vs. Grayson Waller
  • Hosted by Pretty Deadly

WrestleZone will have complete coverage of the event as it airs on April 1.
