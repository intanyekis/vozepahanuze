Update: Final Teams Announced for PWG DDT4 Tournament: First-Round Matches Set

Pro Wrestling Guerilla has named the final two teams, as well as all four first round matches for the upcoming DDT4 Tournament.  

1. The Briscoe Brothers vs. Kevin Steen & El Generico

2. Eddie Edwards & Roderick Strong vs. Adam Cole & Kyle O'Reilly

3. Ricochet & Rich Swann vs. The Young Bucks

4. Michael Elgin & Brian Cage vs. The Super Smash Bros. 

That means not only one, but two bitter rivals will reform their tag teams for the upcoming show on January 12. Visit ProWrestlingGuerilla.com for tickets and more information.
