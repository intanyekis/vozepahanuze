Cameron Cruz

American actor, Cameron Cruz is popular for his role as a sailor in the musical action-adventure movie, The LXD: The Legion of Extraordinary Dancers. He is also the director of operations at the AMIRI, which is a luxurious man and womenswear. He frequently appears on popular TV shows and recently starred on the horror TV show, Dark/Web.

Cruz was born as Cameron Connerty on 23rd February 1987, in the United States. He, moreover, holds an American nationality and hails from white ethnicity. So, what's happening in Cruz's life, is he dating someone or he is single right now? Take a look at Cruz's personal life in the below.

Cameron's Relationship with Jesse Egan

CHIPS star, Cruz is currently dating an American filmmaker and actor, Jesse Egan. The duo first kept their relationship secret to their fans, but later, they came out to be a gay couple when Cameron posted a photo of Jesse and him celebrating national boyfriend day on 4th October 2018.

Cruz also wished Egan on his birthday on 18th November 2019 and shared an awesome picture on Instagram. As of now, there are no disputes arise in their relationship and maintains a blissful bond.

Cruz's Past Relationship & Affairs

Fame actor, Cruz was first linked into a romantic relationship with Monica Padman, an actress. The duo first met on the set of HeILA in 2014, and after working for a year, they started dating.

The duo, unfortunately, broke up due to some personal conflict. Monica also shared a picture of her and Cameron, and in the caption, she proclaimed that they ended their relationship on 17th April 2015.

Furthermore, Cameron was involved in an affair with Michael Nardelli, an actor, and producer. The duo had their first encounter on the set of Circle in 2015, and shortly they get interacted with each other.

After working together for two years, they went out for their first date on 22nd March 2017 at the TCL Chinese Theatres in Los Angeles, California. Later, they, however, ended their relationship but maintains a good friendship.

Upon their separation, Cruz was linked with an American comedian, Ryan Hansen. They were also seen spending quality time at Treasure Island Hotel & Casino in Las Vegas, Nevada, on 18th May 2017. Nevertheless, they also broke after dating for a few months.
