Hispanic vine maker, Lejuan James exchange wedding vows with Instagram model, Camilaa Inc.

LeJuan James is no new name to the world of Instagram. He is a huge social media sensation who has a fan following of over two Million as of 2021. He has a fan following all over the world who are crazy about his unique style of presenting videos and photos.

James grew his popularity on Vine where he created hilarious videos of making fun of his own Hispanic heritage. The video went so viral that it is viewed by Millions and Millions of people from all around the world. Then, he started creating more and more vines related to Hispanics.

LeJuan was born on 19th February 1990 in Puerto Rico. He is currently thirty-one years old. While his wife, Camila was born on 23rd March 1992 in Saint Croix, Virgin Islands. She is currently twenty-nine years old.

Engaged with the girlfriend, Camilaa Inc. in New Year 

Lejuan met his girlfriend, Camilaa Inc. back in 2014 through social media. Camila is also a huge internet sensation who has a hefty amount of fan following. Beautiful wedding ceremony
