Leon Hall is an American professional football player with an estimated net worth of $31 million.

Leon Hall started his career at Vista High School as a wide receiver and defensive back for its football team. In college, he played for Michigan Wolverines and earned an All-American consensus honors.

During the 2007 NFL Draft, he was selected as the first round, 18th overall pick by the Cincinnati Bengals. Hall was named as Second team All-Pro in 2009 and was a recipient of the Ed Block Courage Award in 2012.

During the 2012-2013 season, he appeared in 14 games, did 23 tackles, 14 assists, two interceptions, and one touchdowns.
