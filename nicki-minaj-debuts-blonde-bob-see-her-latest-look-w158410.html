Nicki Minaj Debuts Blonde Bob See Her Latest Look

Nicki Minaj is back to blonde. The songstress debuted her new platinum bob via Instagram on Monday, Nov. 23. “Grizz said when my hair is blonde, I act ‘blonde.’ she's bak bitch,” the “Anaconda” rapper captioned a mirror selfie.

Her new look comes after she opted for straight, dark locks at the 2015 AMAs on Sunday, Nov. 22, where she took home awards for Favorite Hip-Hop Artist and Favorite Hip-Hop Album.

This isn’t the first time that Minaj, 32, has dyed her hair blonde. Back in September, the rapper, who is dating Meek Mill, rocked long ombre locks with thick bangs. She also donned a blonde wig in her “Throw Some Mo” music video.
