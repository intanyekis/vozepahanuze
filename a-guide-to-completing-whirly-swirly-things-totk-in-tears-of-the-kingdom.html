A Guide to Completing Whirly Swirly Things TotK in Tears Of The Kingdom

About The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Dive into the mesmerizing world of The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, an action-packed adventure that will keep you at the edge of your seat. Released by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch in 2023, this thrilling sequel to The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild takes players on an exhilarating journey through the expansive open world of Hyrule.

Prepare for an unforgettable adventure as you join the legendary hero, Link, on a quest to save Hyrule alongside Princess Zelda. Their adversary, the powerful Ganondorf, seeks to bring chaos to the kingdom, and your mission is to thwart his evil plans. Tears of the Kingdom started as DLC for Breath of the Wild but evolved into a full-fledged sequel with an expanded scale that exceeded expectations.

Under the leadership of director Hidemaro Fujibayashi and producer Eiji Aonuma, the development team at Nintendo’s Entertainment Planning & Development division poured their passion and creativity into crafting a remarkable gaming experience. The game’s release in May 2023 shattered records, selling over 10 million copies in just three days.

Discovering Whirly Swirly Things in Tears of the Kingdom

In this quest, Kula the Korok assigns Link the task of capturing photos of the breathtaking whirlpools scattered across Hyrule. These mesmerizing natural wonders can be seen both in the water and on the sandy shores. Kula, being confined to the forest, relies on Link’s documentation to confirm the existence of these captivating phenomena.

While numerous water whirlpools can be found throughout the game, the easiest one to locate is on the eastern side of Lake Hylia. If Link has already found the Popla Foothills Skyview Tower, they can teleport there and glide to the designated location.

Steps to Complete Whirly Swirly Things in Tears of the Kingdom

Follow these steps to complete the Whirly Swirly Things quest in Tears of the Kingdom:

  • Start by finding a whirlpool in different locations across Hyrule. Notable examples include the Zora region whirlpools at East Reservoir Lake and Lake Hylia.
  • Take a photo of the whirlpool using the Camera on the Purah Pad. Ensure there is a red exclamation mark next to the whirlpool’s name tag in the photo.
  • Return to Kula with the whirlpool photo, who will then task you with finding a Sand Swirly Whirly thing in Gerudo Desert.

Locating Sand Swirly Whirly Things in Gerudo Desert

To find the Sand Swirly Whirly Things in the Gerudo Desert:

When in Gerudo Desert, search for a quicksand pit, which can be found in various areas across the desert. Specific locations include the bottom right area near Siwakama Shrine and the entrance to Ancient Prison Ruins in Palu Wasteland.

These quicksand pits are essential for completing the quest, and exploring the desert will help you find the Sand Swirly Whirly Thing needed. Good luck on your quest to uncover these mysterious wonders!
