Roseanne Barr Mimics Kim Kardashian in Tight Black Dress: Skinny Photo

You like what you see, Kanye? Roseanne Barr proudly showed off her drastic weight loss via Twitter on Wednesday, April 23. The comedian-actress tried to get Kanye West's attention by mimicking fiancee Kim Kardashian's sexy, bootylicious pose in a tight-fitted black dress.

"Hey! #Kanye," the 61-year-old wrote. "@Rosie check out my shit! Haute hot and hawt," she tweeted to Rosie O'Donnell.

In the snapshot, Barr gave a side-view of her slimmed-down figure as she tilted her head to the left. She topped off the revealing look by throwing her locks up in a messy bun and wearing no makeup.

The Roseanne alum seems to have embraced her new thin frame since attending the NBCUniversal Summer Press Day in Pasadena, Calif., on April 8. At the event, Barr looked unrecognizable (and was all smiles!) as she walked the red carpet in a fitted black top and matching black pants.
