Keith Hernandez Net Worth - NewsNow Nigeria

Keith Hernandez has a net worth estimated at $15 million. He was born in San Francisco, California, on October 20, 1953. Hernandez played Major League Baseball for teams including the New York Mets and St. Louis Cardinals. He is now a popular baseball analyst and broadcaster. His net worth has been largely accumulated from his successful baseball career, as well as from his ventures in writing, acting, and commercial endorsements.

During his high school years at Terra Nova High School and later at Capuchino High School, Hernandez’s baseball skills started gaining attention. He was drafted by the St. Louis Cardinals in the 1971 Major League Baseball Draft and notably became a star batter, eventually being named the National League’s Most Valuable Player. His career reached its peak when he helped the Cardinals win the 1982 World Series and later contributed to the New York Mets’ 1986 World Series win.

Following his retirement from professional baseball, Hernandez delved into writing and acting. He also became a baseball analyst for SNY and WPIX in 2006, gaining popularity for his unique commentary style.

Apart from his professional endeavors, Keith Hernandez is known for his charismatic personality and iconic mustache. He has been married to Kai since 2005, and he previously was married to Susan, with whom he divorced in 1987.
